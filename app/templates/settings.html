Real-time polling

Check the inbox for newly-arrived mail and scan it within seconds — near real-time, instead of waiting for the next scheduled audit. Each new message is classified, phishing-checked, and labeled just like an audit; the results show up as a scan in the audit list. Scheduled audits stay on as a backstop.
